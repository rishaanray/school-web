  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    1.Accurracy
    Humans are prone to error, even if we provide them with supporting computationtal devices. Automatated attendance management system ensure accurate time records and minimize the invetiable and costly error with manual data entry. This accurate data thereby help to provide accurate perfomence and payroll data.

    2.Economy
    Have you ever thought that attendance management system could control costs? It saves money by putting an end to inaccurate time reporting, buddy punching, absenteesism, tardiness, time abuse, and over payment. As a matter of fact, a study done by Axsium group food found thta large onganization save up to $1600 per employee after inplementing automated attendance managment system.

    3.Productivity/ Efficiency
    Monitoring and managing attendance manually can be a time-consumin,labourious, and expensive affair. It takes time to process paper sheets and time cards, create schedule, authorize leave and overtime, and create payroll manually. Free up precious administration time with an automated system that does it all for you,from keep track of employee hours to automatically importing information into your payroll system.The time and effort saved combined with data accuracy helps in optimizing the use of resources which lead to increased productivity and improves profit.
